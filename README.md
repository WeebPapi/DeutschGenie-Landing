# DeutschGenie Landing Page by Aleksandre Kapanadze

## Research notes

While researching potential keywords and assets to be used in the landing page, I came to the conclusion that the key to finding the right keywords would be to balance appealing text like "Learn German online" with more specific keywords like "Visual German learning app" or "German Learning App" to stand out from competitors while also appealing to a portion of their audience.

Given the app's focus on visual, gamified learning and the existing appeal and familiarity of our target audience with short form content, the keywords should also reflect this by including terms like "Quick German lessons", "Fun German learning" and "Learn German fast"

It would also be a good idea to focus on the structured nature of the app by using keywords like "A1 to C2 German course" or "German Placement Test" as anyone looking for these keywords is likely to be interested in an app like DeutschGenie.

In the end, the main keywords I want to use are:

- Learn German online
- Visual German learning app
- Learn German with games
- German games
- Quick German lessons
- Fun German learning
- Learn German fast
- A1 to C2 German course
- German Placement Test

## Assets and design notes

I want the landing page to be visually appealing and to leave an impression, especially given our target audience who is very comfortable with browsing online and is familiar with other memorable aesthetics.

The page should utilize the brand colors of orange/grey with blue accents and the aesthetic should be a bit playful but also trustworthy and high quality, a good option could be a neobrutalist design with a grid background, similar to https://www.neobrutalism.dev/

The assets used should be high quality, leverage seo pracices like alt text and semantic HTML like figures and figcaptions. The images themselves should contain elements that evoke feelings of fun, learning, socializing and becoming adept at speaking German. Additionally, it could help to have relevant images and copy text for the different users who might be interested in this app, such as students going on exchange programs, starting studies abroad or just looking to improve their skills.

Possible asset ideas:

- Student boarding plane to Germany
- Students sitting in a relaxed environment, chatting and laughing
- Person smiling and looking at their phone on a train
- Person ordering a coffee
- Person getting a döner kebab with friends
